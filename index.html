Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that allows peer-to-peer transactions without intermediaries.

How do I buy Bitcoin?

You can buy Bitcoin through exchanges, peer-to-peer platforms, or Bitcoin ATMs. You can also exchange Bitcoin directly with others securely.

Bitcoin Made Simple ₿

Bitcoin is like digital money that you can use to buy things or save for later. Here’s how you can get Bitcoin and keep it safe:

Step 1: Get Bitcoin

  1. Find a place to buy Bitcoin (like a special app or website).
  2. Use your money (like dollars) to buy Bitcoin.
  3. Wait for the Bitcoin to show up in your digital wallet.

Step 2: Keep Your Bitcoin Safe

  1. Get a special wallet (like a piggy bank, but for Bitcoin).
  2. Write down your secret code (called a "seed phrase") and keep it in a safe place.
  3. Never share your secret code with anyone!
