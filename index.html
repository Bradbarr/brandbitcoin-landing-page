Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that allows peer-to-peer transactions without intermediaries.

How do I buy Bitcoin?

You can buy Bitcoin through exchanges, peer-to-peer platforms, or Bitcoin ATMs. You can also exchange Bitcoin directly with others securely.

Bitcoin Basics ₿

Bitcoin is a revolutionary digital asset that offers financial freedom and security. Whether you're new to Bitcoin or looking to deepen your understanding, here's a simple guide to get started:

Step 1: Acquire Bitcoin

  1. Choose a Platform: Use a trusted exchange or peer-to-peer platform to buy Bitcoin.
  2. Make a Purchase: Use your local currency to buy Bitcoin securely.
  3. Receive Bitcoin: Your Bitcoin will be sent to your digital wallet.

Step 2: Secure Your Bitcoin

  1. Set Up a Wallet: Choose a secure wallet to store your Bitcoin.
  2. Backup Your Wallet: Write down your recovery phrase and store it in a safe place.
  3. Protect Your Keys: Never share your private keys or recovery phrase with anyone.
